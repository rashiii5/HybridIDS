What it does

Four-stage intelligent pipeline

Each component is independently valuable — together they form a robust, explainable detection system.

🧠

Self-Supervised Learning

A Variational Autoencoder trained exclusively on normal traffic learns the latent structure of benign behavior — without any attack labels.

🚨

Adaptive Anomaly Detection

Reconstruction error is log-transformed and compared against an ensemble threshold derived from three complementary methods.

🎯

Attack Classification

A cost-sensitive LightGBM classifier — trained with SMOTE-ENN balancing — categorises flagged traffic into DoS, Probe, R2L, or U2R.

🔍

SHAP Explainability

Every flagged sample comes with a SHAP breakdown showing exactly which features drove the decision — in plain English.

⚖️

Imbalance-Aware

SMOTE-ENN oversampling combined with 12× cost-sensitive weights for U2R — the rarest and most critical attack class.

📊

Full Model Comparison

Four experiments compared head-to-head: supervised baseline, VAE-only, basic hybrid, and the full cost-sensitive system.
